Fun Games to Learn the Constitution!

Discover exciting and interactive games that make learning about the Constitution fun and engaging. Test your knowledge and have a blast!

Game 1: Constitution Maze

Navigate through a maze filled with constitutional challenges to find your way to the Constitution Hall. Can you make it through?

Game 2: Rights Matching

Match each right to its corresponding amendment in this educational game. Improve your understanding of your rights!

Game 3: Constitution Quiz Show

Participate in a quiz show format to test your knowledge about the Constitution. Compete for high scores and learn new facts!

Game 4: Amendment Adventure

Embark on an adventure to discover how different amendments have changed history. Solve puzzles and learn along the way!
